Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Lexi-Fair AI, we may collect data that you provide directly to us (such as your account details, configurations, and uploaded datasets) as well as data that is collected automatically (such as usage metrics and error logs). We prioritize minimal data collection, ensuring that your proprietary datasets are processed securely and locally whenever possible.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is strictly used to provide, maintain, and improve our AI bias detection services. This includes performing intersectional audits, generating fairness reports, and communicating essential updates regarding the platform. We do not use your proprietary dataset content for training our own models without explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information and datasets against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. We adhere to global compliance standards, including GDPR and the EU AI Act, to ensure your data remains secure at all times.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Lexi-Fair AI does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or proprietary datasets to third parties. We may share limited anonymized data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, provided those parties agree to keep the information confidential.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
